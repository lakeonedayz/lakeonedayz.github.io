About LakeONE

Our Server

LakeONE is a dedicated DayZ server offering a balanced mix of PvP and PvE gameplay. Set in the hauntingly beautiful Chernarus, our server provides a challenging survival experience with custom mods, active admins, and a vibrant community.

Features

  • Custom loot tables for a unique experience
  • Regular events and community challenges
  • 24/7 uptime with active moderation
  • Faction system for team-based gameplay
  • Custom bases and trading zones
